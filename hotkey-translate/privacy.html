Privacy Policy for HotKey Translate

Last updated: May 6, 2026

HotKey Translate is a macOS menu bar app for quick text translation. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data Collection

HotKey Translate does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data with the developer. The app does not operate its own servers.

Clipboard Text

The app reads text from the macOS clipboard only when you activate the configured hotkey or open the translator. This text is used to fill the translation field in the embedded Google Translate web view.

HotKey Translate does not store clipboard contents and does not transmit clipboard contents to the developer.

Third-Party Service

Translations are performed through Google Translate in an embedded web view. Google may process data according to its own privacy policy. HotKey Translate is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google.

Local Preferences

The app may store local preferences on your device, such as the selected hotkey and launch-at-login setting. These preferences are not transmitted to the developer.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository: HotKey Translate Issues.
